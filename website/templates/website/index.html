Stroke Monitoring System


The stroke risk recommender system is meant for doctors and patients to view the patient's risk of having a stroke. On the website, there is a chart and a graph displaying the main factors that could cause a stroke and how much each poses as a risk towards the patient. The patient can then download the results as a pdf.

Heart Monitoring System


The smart heart monitoring system guides both doctors and patients on the patient's risk of having a heart attack just by downloading an installation by wearing a smartwatch. The recommended smartwatches are the Apple Watch or the Samsung Smart Watch. There is also a website in which the doctor and the patient can view a chart and a graph displaying the main factors that could cause a heart attack and which ones to be more careful about. The patient can also download the results from the website as a pdf.
